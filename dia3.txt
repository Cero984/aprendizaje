I learned:
chmod means Change Mode. Its function is to change 
the permissions of a file.
You can set read, write, or execute permissions, or a combination.
To see the permissions, use "ls -l". 
This shows the permissions for the owner, group, and others.
It also shows the creation date, the owner, 
the group, and the assigned permissions.
